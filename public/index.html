How do you remember every detail of the potential homes you have seen and not get overwhelmed? Answer is Cast-Around. An app that will help you store what you liked or didn't like about the house you have seen. Its a quick 3 step process

Step One: Make a list of home that you want to check out with zillow powered search.

Step Two: Actually go and view the houses in your list either at an open house or with a realtor.

Step Three: Add your notes to the houses you saw, what you liked, what you didn't like, potential offer price that you would like to make etc.

You can create organized profile of the houses you saw in your house-hunting , even by giving them nick names! It's that easy. Ready to give it a try?
